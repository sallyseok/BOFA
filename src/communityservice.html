COMMUNITY SERVICE


Cranes for Care

Cranes for Care is an event where our club meets together to fold cranes and write encouragement notes for hospitalized patients. We send our cranes to the Ronald Reagan UCLA Medical Center to include in their regular care packages to give to their lonely or depressed patients. One of the main visions of our club is to make an impact on our community with origami. With our origami cranes, a symbol of hope and peace, we hope to uplift the spirits of those who receive them and bring a little bit of joy in uncertain times.
